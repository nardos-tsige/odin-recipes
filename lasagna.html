Lasagna

Description

This classic lasagna recipe is made with layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and a blend of three cheeses. It's the ultimate comfort food that feeds a crowd and tastes even better the next day.

Originating from Italy, this hearty dish has become a family favorite worldwide. The secret to this recipe is slow-simmering the sauce to develop deep flavors before layering and baking to golden perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 (6.5 ounce) cans canned tomato sauce
  • 2 (6 ounce) cans tomato paste
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 16 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 3/4 pound mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, salt, pepper, and Italian seasoning. Simmer covered for 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles for 8-10 minutes. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, parsley, and salt.
  5. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  6. Spread 1.5 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over sauce. Spread half the ricotta mixture over noodles. Top with 1/3 of mozzarella slices and 1/4 cup Parmesan.
  7. Repeat layers and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake another 25 minutes. Let rest for 15 minutes before serving.
