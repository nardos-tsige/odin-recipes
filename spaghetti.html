Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

This classic Italian pasta dish comes from Rome and is made with eggs, hard cheese, cured pork, and black pepper. It's creamy, rich, and incredibly satisfying.

The key to perfect carbonara is tossing the hot pasta with the egg mixture off the heat to create a silky sauce without scrambling the eggs.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 8 ounces pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook spaghetti according to package directions.
  2. While pasta cooks, fry pancetta in a large skillet until crispy. Remove from heat.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs, cheese, and plenty of black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving 1/2 cup pasta water.
  5. Quickly toss hot pasta with pancetta, then add egg mixture and toss rapidly. Add pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  6. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
