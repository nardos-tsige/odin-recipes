Garlic Chicken

Description

This creamy garlic chicken features tender pan-seared chicken breasts in a rich, flavorful garlic cream sauce. It's elegant enough for company but easy enough for a weeknight dinner.

The sauce is made with fresh garlic, heavy cream, and Parmesan cheese, creating a luxurious coating for the chicken.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped

Steps

  1. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook chicken until golden and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same skillet, add garlic and cook for 1 minute until fragrant.
  4. Pour in chicken broth and cream, scraping up browned bits from the pan. Simmer until sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and smooth.
  6. Return chicken to the skillet and simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  7. Garnish with parsley before serving.
